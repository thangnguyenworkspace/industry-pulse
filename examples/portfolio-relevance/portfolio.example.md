# Portfolio (example)

A small illustrative portfolio used as the context object. Replace the holdings and theses with your own, or point the prompt at a different file entirely. Nothing here is financial advice; the tickers are placeholders chosen to show the format.

| Ticker | Weight | Thesis (why you hold it — what you want signal on) |
|---|---|---|
| NVDA | 30% | AI-compute demand stays ahead of supply; watch for capacity, competition, and any demand-softening signal. |
| MSFT | 20% | Enterprise AI adoption via Copilot + Azure; watch agentic-product traction and cloud-AI pricing pressure. |
| Cash | 25% | Dry powder. Watch for macro / rate signals that change the deployment calculus. |
| A generic AI-infra name | 15% | The "picks and shovels" bet beyond the megacaps; watch for the agent-infra layer commoditizing or consolidating. |
| A generic fintech name | 10% | Payments + regulated-crypto rails; watch stablecoin regulation and tokenization adoption. |

## How to read this against a brief

For each holding, the question the relevance pass answers is: *did anything in today's brief change the thesis, confirm it, or threaten it?* A holding with no relevant signal is skipped — silence is a valid result. The `Cash` and macro/regulatory theses are deliberately included to show that a context object can hold non-equity positions whose "signal" is a market or policy read.
