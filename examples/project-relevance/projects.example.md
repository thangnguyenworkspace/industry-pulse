# Projects (example)

A set of illustrative projects used as context objects, one block each. Replace them with your own, active projects, products, a startup thesis, research threads. The relevance pass fans out one sub-agent per block, so add or remove blocks freely.

Each block gives the agent enough to judge relevance: what the project is, what stage it is at, and what kinds of signal would actually matter to it.

---

## acme-agent-platform

**What it is:** a developer platform for building and running multi-agent workflows. Pre-launch, building the orchestration + eval layer.

**Signal that matters:** anything on agent orchestration frameworks, eval/observability tooling, context-management patterns, and competitor launches in the agent-infra space. Pricing and adoption moves by the model labs. Less relevant: consumer AI, macro, fintech.

---

## northwind-gtm-service

**What it is:** a productized go-to-market service that wires AI agents into outbound and lead-gen for B2B teams. Early revenue, scaling delivery.

**Signal that matters:** GTM-engineering practice, outbound/lead-gen tooling, AI applied to sales motion, and how AI-native companies structure growth. Buyer-side signal (what B2B teams are adopting). Less relevant: deep model research, hardware.

---

## sidebet-research

**What it is:** a personal research thread on prediction markets as a financial-signal source. Exploratory, no product.

**Signal that matters:** prediction-market mechanics and adoption, event-probability data as signal, and the regulatory picture around them. Less relevant: most AI-tooling and GTM signal.
